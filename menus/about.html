Welcome

We are delighted to welcome you to Pajiro Memorial where our outstanding location and warm family atmosphere have been welcoming students over the years.

By offering experiences to build resilience, we prepare our students for successful and balanced lives. Our teaching standard and sports programmes provide them with challenges to develop personal confidence.

We value academic excellence and our highly experienced and passionate teachers focus on inquiry-based learning, using state-of-the-art technology. Our small class sizes allow students to blossom in an optimal and creative environment.
