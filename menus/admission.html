Admission Overview

Pajiro Memorial has been one of the leading day schools since its foundation in 2016. Each year we welcome students from different places.

We provide a secure environment and an extraordinary education for students from 5 to 18 years old. We provide individual attention to each student, encouraging a passion for learning and ensuring the rounded development of all the young people in our care academically, socially and emotionally.

Pajiro Memorial prides itself on its long and rich history as an English-based place of learning. While the main language of academic subjects is English, we believe that language learning is vitally important and students are encouraged to develop a mastery of the French Language in addition to studying their own language.

Above all, we offer a safe and healthy environment in which every child feels cherished. For more information or for any admissions enquiries please email admission@pajiromemorial.com.ng and we will be in touch quickly.

Admission Process

Pajiro Memorial gives admission to brilliant young boys and girls every year. Suitable qualified candidates are given admission after admission interview.

The admission form is free and can be filled online, downloaded or can be obtained from the school: office of the Principal.
