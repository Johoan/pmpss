Welcome

Pajiro Memorial welcomes young people aged 5 to 18 from more than different places to our exceptional school in Umuchuru Itu Ezinihitte Mbaise. We pride ourselves on our bilingual approach, giving students mastery of French and English. Our students follow globally accredited programmes of study, creating informed, globally competent, and future-ready learners. Pajiro Memorial core values are academic excellence, a close-knit family atmosphere, security and cultural diversity.

Our Motto

“Ferant Secum Scientia et Artes”
"Empowering with Knowledge and Skills"

Our Mission

To develop an enduring high standard School (school of class)
that can compete favourably well with other forward looking
schools in contemporary world of dynamic education in science
and technology at affordable cost-benefits to beneficiaries of
the school.

Our Vision

To produce men and women of integrity, fortified
with necessary intellectual tools, technical know-how,
who will harness our rich natural resources, transform
them into public wealth and pilot the affairs of the
nation and world at large into an “El-dorado” of our
time – (a dream come true) where everybody would be
gainfully and comfortably occupied.
